The Château de Goville



Located in the heart of Normandy, the Château de Goville is an architectural jewel dating back to the 18th century. Surrounded by a lush park and French-style gardens, this historic estate has been lovingly restored to offer its guests an authentic and refined experience. Once a summer residence to an aristocratic family, the château has stood the test of time while preserving its charm and elegance. Today, we invite you to discover the French art of living in an exceptional setting, by either booking a private room, or renting out the entire property.

We'll warmly welcome you and advise you through your stay, whether you come for a romantic night, a family weekend, or a special event reception.
