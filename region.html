Discover Normandy



Located in the heart of Normandy, the Château de Goville is a perfect base to explore one of France's most captivating regions. Normandy is a land of contrasts, where dramatic white cliffs meet peaceful apple orchards, and medieval towns stand alongside the D-Day landing beaches. Whether you're interested in history, nature, gastronomy, or simply relaxing in beautiful surroundings, Normandy offers an array of experiences that will make your stay at Château de Goville truly unforgettable.

Discover Our Region

D-Day Landing Beaches

Walk in the footsteps of history along the famous D-Day beaches where Allied forces landed in 1944. Visit museums, memorials, and cemeteries that pay tribute to this pivotal moment in world history.

30 min drive

Mont Saint-Michel

Discover the magical island commune of Mont Saint-Michel, a UNESCO World Heritage site. This breathtaking abbey seems to float on the water during high tide, creating an unforgettable spectacle.

1h30 drive

Christian Dior Museum

Step into the world of haute couture at the childhood home of Christian Dior. This elegant villa and its magnificent seaside gardens, known as "Les Rhumbs," were the legendary designer's first source of inspiration.

1h20 drive

Historic Honfleur

Explore the charming port town of Honfleur with its picturesque harbor, narrow streets, and half-timbered houses. This artist's haven inspired many Impressionist painters including Monet.

1h drive

Bayeux Tapestry

Marvel at the extraordinary Bayeux Tapestry, an 11th-century embroidered cloth nearly 70 meters long that depicts the events leading up to the Norman conquest of England.

20 min drive

Étretat Cliffs

Experience the dramatic white chalk cliffs and natural arches of Étretat, a spectacular coastline that has inspired artists and writers for centuries. Enjoy breathtaking walks along the clifftops.

1h45 drive
