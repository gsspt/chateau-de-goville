Our Rooms


Discover our exquisite rooms, historically preserved and fitted out with modern comfort. Available from the 1st of June to the 31st of August, through Booking, Airbnb or by contacting the owner directly.

Chambre Député

Double Room with Shower

This elegantly appointed room is named after Député Gosset, member of parliament, who built the castle during the French Revolution in 1789. It features a canopy bed, antique furnishings, and a collection of revolutionary engravings. The room overlooks the courtyard and offers a peaceful retreat.

Book Now

Chambre Commandant

Double Room with Shower

Named after a distinguished military commander who once resided here, this spacious room features high ceilings, original hardwood floors, and a luxurious en-suite bathroom with marble finishes. The large windows offer a stunning view of the castle gardens.

Book Now

Chambre Reine Hortense

Double Room with Bath

This majestic room is named after Queen Hortense, who allegedly stayed here during her travels through Normandy. The room features a luxurious freestanding bathtub, silk draperies, and a canopy bed. The elegant decor pays homage to the room's royal history.

Book Now

Chambre Madeleine

Double Room with Bath

Named after the castle's original owner's daughter, this charming room combines rustic elegance with modern comforts. Its red decoration and view of the French garden create a cozy atmosphere.
